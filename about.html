About Us

We are the Asia oldest University

Aliah University, formerly known as the Mohammedan College of Calcutta, is a prestigious educational institution located in Kolkata, West Bengal, India. Established in 1780, it has a rich history and has evolved into a modern university offering a wide range of academic programs. Aliah University is known for its focus on promoting excellence in education while nurturing a strong sense of community and inclusivity. It is committed to providing quality education to students from diverse backgrounds and fostering a spirit of intellectual curiosity and social responsibility.
